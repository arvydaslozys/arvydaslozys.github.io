Tung Tung Tung Sahur (properly Tung Tung Tung Tung Tung Tung Tung Tung Tung Sahur) is an Indonesian brainrot character published on the 28th of February, 2025 by @noxaasht. Tung Tung Tung Sahur is an anthropomorphic log who wields a baseball bat as a weapon.

According to the sound, if you are called for Sahur (an early breakfast in Ramadan) and do not respond, then he will come for you
